Understanding Biometric Authentication

What is Biometric Authentication?

A security process that relies on unique biological characteristics to verify someone's identity.

Types of Biometrics

Physiological (fingerprint, face, iris) and behavioral (typing rhythm, voice, gait) characteristics.

Benefits & Drawbacks

Enhanced security but with privacy concerns and potential for spoofing.
